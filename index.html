OPTIMUS ENGINEERING

We have experience in small and medium-sized buildings, bridge design, maritime structures, prestressed structures, geotehnical design, nonlinear analyses and seismic design of structures. Particular attention during the design we give to the feasibility of our facilities, as well as the quality and quantity of materials and construction cost price.

Structural

ENGINEERING

Our design is mostly based on Eurocodes. For analyses we use various programs in structural engineering, depending on the type of facility, to have the best correspondence with reality.

EARTHQUAKE

ENGINEERING

The seismic stability of the new facilities we ensure through Eurocode 8. Fragility and seismic resistance of existing facilities we can estimate on the basis of nonlinear analysis and real hapend earthquakes.
